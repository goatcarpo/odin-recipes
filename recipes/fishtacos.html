Fish tacos

Description recipe

This recipe for a delicious mexican dish is made with fish

Ingredients

  • fish
  • shredded cabbage
  • hard or soft taco shells
  • beans (black)
  • Shredded cheese
  • lettuce
  • onion

Steps

  1. Make sauce with sour cream, mayonnaise and lime juice
  2. Cut and prepare the vegetables
  3. Warm the hard taco shells
  4. Season fish
  5. Cook the fish
  6. Serve the fish tacos
